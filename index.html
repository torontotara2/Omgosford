OMGosford Business Network

Connecting Businesses. Strengthening Gosford. Shaping Our City’s Future.

Why the OMGosford Business Network Exists

Gosford is a beautiful old town with so much potential — and the businesses here are at the heart of its future. The OMGosford Business Network brings local operators, building owners, entrepreneurs, creatives, and community leaders together to help revitalise the city centre and promote Gosford as the vibrant coastal hub it deserves to be.

A safer, more attractive, more prosperous Gosford — built together.

What We Do

1. Support Local Business

We showcase Gosford’s cafés, shops, services, makers, and attractions through:

  • Feature listings on OMGosford.com
  • Social media spotlights
  • Tourism guides that highlight local operators
  • Community-first messaging that drives foot traffic into town

2. Improve the City Centre

We advocate for shared priorities such as:

  • Streetscape improvements
  • Better lighting and public safety
  • Pedestrian-friendly design
  • Cleaner, greener spaces
  • Signage, beautification, and public art
  • Support for small business growth

3. Build Partnerships That Matter

We work collaboratively with:

  • Local government
  • Property owners
  • Regional tourism organisations
  • Chambers and neighbouring precincts
  • Arts and cultural groups
  • Event organisers

4. Activate Gosford with Events & Experiences

We support initiatives like:

  • Markets
  • Live music
  • Exhibitions
  • Micro-festivals
  • Seasonal celebrations
  • Pop-ups
  • Cultural activities
  • Night-time economy projects

Our Vision

A thriving, walkable, welcoming city centre where businesses flourish, locals feel proud, and visitors are excited to explore. A city that values its heritage — while embracing fresh ideas. A place where collaboration replaces competition, and growth benefits everyone.

Why Your Business Should Join

Membership in the OMGosford Business Network means being part of the movement to reshape Gosford’s future. You'll enjoy:

  • Promotion through the OMGosford platform
  • Networking with other local operators
  • A voice in advocacy and planning
  • Opportunities to participate in events
  • Early access to precinct projects
  • Visibility to visitors, media, and stakeholders
  • Support for your business through a central hub
